Privacy Policy | Arzapress

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Arzapress is an Arabic multi-category online newspaper. This policy explains what data we collect to run accounts, comments, likes, bookmarks (reading list), contributor submissions, and support/report forms.

We collect:
- Account data (email, display name, optional avatar).
- User-generated content (comments, contributor articles submitted for review).
- Engagement actions (likes, bookmarks).
- Basic technical logs for security and reliability.

We do not sell user data. Public pages may show your display name and avatar next to your comments, and contributor names may appear on contributor pages if their articles are published.
