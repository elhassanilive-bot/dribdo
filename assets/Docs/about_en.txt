About Arzapress

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Arzapress is an Arabic multi-category online newspaper. Our mission is to publish clear, well-structured articles and help readers navigate categories and archives easily.

What you can do on Arzapress:
- Read articles across multiple categories.
- Save articles to read later.
- Like and comment (with moderation rules).
- Submit articles as a contributor (reviewed before publishing).

We value transparency. If we correct a material mistake, we aim to update the content and keep the experience trustworthy.
